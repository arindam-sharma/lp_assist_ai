AI Assist for Ultrasound Guided Infant Lumbar Puncture

Note
Your uploaded image will be temporarily stored on a secure cloud server and deleted right after you click the 'Reset' button. Please ensure that there is no patient-identifying information in the image.
This model is designed to evaluate ultrasound images obtained with a linear transducer that show a cross-section (axial view) of the lumbar spinal interspace in an infant (as shown below). It has not been tested for other probes, views, or age groups.

Instructions:

  1. Upload an ultrasound image with given specifications.
  2. Click on "Identify" to process the image.
  3. The model will evaluate the image and identify if the spinal cord and/or spinal fluid is present in the picture.
  4. The model will also check if the image quality is insufficient for evaluation.
